WHAT WE DO!?

As the Entrepreneurship Cell of one of the premier institutes of the country, the prime focus of the organization is to provide an environment conducive to promoting the entrepreneurial spirit and related skills among not only the students of the college but all across the nation.

WE INSPIRE

Facilitate entrepreneurial activity on campus in various ways. Encourage students for Entrepreneurship and maintain a quality culture of entrepreneurial guidance.

WE ASPIRE

We aspire to cater to the interests of a wide variety of people - ranging from students with an entrepreneurial bent to economically weaker sections in the nearby rural areas, as well as established startups in need of funding or mentorship.

WE PERSPIRE

Bridge the gap between industry and the academic curriculum by organising various talk shows by leading entrepreneurs and leading industry experts and industry standard workshop and panel discussion regarding various topics.
